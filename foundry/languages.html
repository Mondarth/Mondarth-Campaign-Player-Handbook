LanguagesÔÉÅ

Your character may be able to speak and read different languages to other party members. Sometimes you will see or hear things they cannot understand.

In FVTT if your character understands the language a message is given in you will automatically receive the translation. If your character does not understand the language you will see the message only in the original language.
